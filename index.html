About Me

Hello! My name is Anawin, I am a Los Angeles/Orange County-based Software Engineer.


Growing up, I have always been fascinated in the realms of science and art. This led me to persue engineering as a career and learn many different art forms as a hobby like graphic design, music, and dance. Once I became introduced to software and web development, I absolutely knew this was something I wanted to persue.

I have had an incredible journey so far. I developed a strong foundation from Thinkful's full Stack Web Development Program and right after completing that program I joined my first Hackathon in LA with a few other new aspiring engineers/developers. I have had the pleasure of working at a small start-up company, Sabai Software, where we had to build everything from the ground up to a big company, LegalZoom, where their processes and codebase have been refined through many years.

When I'm not coding, I find interests in playing and creating music, watching and playing sports, playing team oriented and high-level strategic video games, and exploring the great outdoors. I also love sharing new experiences, discoveries, laughs, and smiles.
